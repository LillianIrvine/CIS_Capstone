## Effect of Artificial Intelligence on Human Creativity and Originality

This work discusses the benefits that the use of artificial intelligence has to offer regarding the individual and their creative expression along with their intellectual exploration and freedom

The paper suggests a definition of an application of originality that will separate the higher levels of creativity in human persons to an extent in which it constitutes a redefinition of creativity, allowing for the use of AI to be considered a new medium or tool for professional use

The work also takes into consideration the common uses of AI and their potential benefits. 

Topics regarding legal concerns and authorship of AI generated content are also mentioned as a growing concern as suggestions of further attention and research be completed
